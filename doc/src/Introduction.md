# Introduction

## MELISA PROJECT

Hey there! 👋 Welcome to **MELISA** – the **M**anagement **E**nvironment **LI**nux **SA**ndbox.

You know that feeling when you want to try out a new programming language, or test a weird library, but you're terrified of messing up your pristine development machine? Or worse: you write code that works perfectly on your laptop, but when your colleague runs it, you get the dreaded *“it works on my machine”* reply?

**MELISA** is here to solve exactly that. It’s a **Rust‑powered tool** that lets you spin up isolated LXC containers in seconds, manage users with fine‑grained permissions, and collaborate on projects without ever polluting your host system.

Think of it as a friendly butler who prepares a clean room for you every time you want to code. You walk in, do your thing, and when you leave – the room is wiped clean (or not, if you want to keep it around).

In this book we’ll explore everything MELISA can do, from installing it on your server to running scripts remotely from your laptop. Grab a coffee ☕ and let’s dive in!
